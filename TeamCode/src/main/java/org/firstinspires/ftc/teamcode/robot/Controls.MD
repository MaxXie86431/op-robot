
# Gamepad 1

## Left Joystick
- Moves the robot
## Right Joystick
- Turns the robot

## X - Square 
- Flick1
## Y - Triangle
- Flick2
## A - Cross/X
- speed toggle for drivetrain
## B - Circle
- Flick 3

## DpadUp
- Turn on/off auto tracking
## DpadDown
- reverse flywheel
## DpadLeft
- Turret left
## DpadRight
- Turret right
## Right Trigger
- Flywheel shoots out + three flickers flick
## Right Bumper
- constant shot as fallback+flicks three
## Left Bumper
- Intake Out
## Left Trigger
- Intake In
